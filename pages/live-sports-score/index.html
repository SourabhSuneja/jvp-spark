Points Criteria:

  • All games and events are divided into three types:
    • Individual Events (example: 100m Race, Hurdle Race)
    • Group Events (example: Soft Board Decoration, Relay Race – pairs of students)
    • Team Events (example: Kho Kho, Kabaddi)
  • Points for Individual & Group Events:
    • 1st place: 10 points
    • 2nd place: 7 points
    • 3rd place: 5 points
  • Points for Team Events (Double Points):
    • 1st place: 20 points
    • 2nd place: 14 points
  • Add points from all events to get the Total Score of a house.
  • Easy Example:
    • A house got:
      • 1st place in 2 individual events: 2 × 10 = 20 points
      • 2nd place in 1 group event: 1 × 7 = 7 points
      • 1st place in 1 team event: 1 × 20 = 20 points
    • Total points: 20 + 7 + 20 = 47 points
  • The Grand Total of each house also includes points from other school activities held during the session (like Soft Board Decoration, Rakhi Making, etc.).
