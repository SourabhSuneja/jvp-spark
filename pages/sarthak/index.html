Your AI Study Buddy

Sarthak is a helpful and child-friendly AI chatbot created by Sourabh sir to answer your school questions. Sarthak can make learning easier for you and can talk to you in both English and Hindi. Here are some example questions you can ask from Sarthak to get started:

  • What is Microsoft Excel?
  • Any easy way to remember the order of planets?
  • Some tips to improve Maths?

Terms and Conditions

When you use Sarthak, you agree to these rules:

  • You will use Sarthak for school work and learning only.
  • Do not use mean or violent words.
  • The answers from Sarthak might be sometimes wrong or irrelevant. Always check with your books or teachers to make sure you get the right information.
  • Do not share any personal details about yourself.
  • Your conversations might be saved to help keep things safe and improve Sarthak.
